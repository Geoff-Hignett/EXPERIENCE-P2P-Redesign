About Us

Based in the United Kingdom, we are an informative provider of peer-to-peer lending news and advice.

EXPERIENCE P2P was conceived in 2018, at a time when peer-to-peer lending was really taking off all around the world. Knowing finance isn't the most interesting of topics for many people, we decided to research the sector and put some solid information out there for the public.

We know that investing in anything can be not only complicated, but an intimidating prospect. We aim to give people confidence as well as knowledge, so the right people can decide if peer-to-peer lending is something for them. We recognise (and tell everyone we speak to) that not investing is the right course for lots of people.

Since 2018, we have been using many of the most popular peer-to-peer lending sites, and comparing earnings, ease of use and customer service. In our reviews we explain how we feel about each product, and what type of investor we would recommend it for.
